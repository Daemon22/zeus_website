Our Story

ZEUS was conceptualised and founded in 2040 by Jax Teller. His father, John Teller, was a U.S. Navy SEAL. He fought in World War 3 and was killed in action by enemy sniper fire while saving a fellow SEAL who had been wounded. It was then that Jax conceptualised an idea to make firearms safer and military equipment, such as kevlar vests, more effective in order to protect people and save lives.

What We Do

ZEUS is a research and development company specializing in providing weapons and combat gear. Our mission is to give the military and private sector security companies the advantage in combat situations, by providing them with specialized equipment that is safer to use, more protective, and more effective. This will allow for the performance of high risk operations and duties to be carried out at a greatly reduced risk of harm to the personnel.

What We Offer

ZEUS offers the most protective and safe combat gear on the market anywhere in the world. Our stores around the world all have access to indoor and outdoor shooting ranges and training facilities. Our instructors are all ex-military and have more than 10 years experience with weapons and safety.
