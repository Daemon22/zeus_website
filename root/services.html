ZEUS has fully equipped indoor and outdoor shooting ranges that can be booked by customers to sharpen and improve their weapons skills. Firearms are also available for rent, along with amunition and protective gear.

Basic Training

ZEUS offers a basic weapons training course that teaches the basics of weapons safety, handling and cleaning. This course takes place in our indoor shooting range and is perfect for anyone wanting to learn how to effectively use a firearm.

Firearms, ammunition and protective equipment are all included. This courses training dates are subject to bookings.

Advanced Training

ZEUS offers an advanced course on weapons training for anyone that has experience with weapons and is looking to further upskill themselves on a tactical level. The training involves clearing rooms and dealing with multiple opponents in confined areas.

Firearms, ammunition and protective equipment are all included. This courses training dates are subject to bookings.
